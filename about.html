The Art of Simplicity.

Our Story

Tradition Meets Purpose.

We believe in doing more with less. Every piece is meticulously crafted by master artisans using sustainably sourced materials, ensuring your everyday carry ages beautifully alongside you.

Zerano started with a simple idea: that design is not decoration, but the visible form of an idea. By stripping away everything non-essential, we focus on material quality, stitching precision, and structural integrity.

Over the years, we have worked intimately with local tanneries, ensuring the highest standards of vegetable tanning without harsh chemicals. This guarantees leather that breathes, adapts, and develops a distinct patina over a lifetime of use.
